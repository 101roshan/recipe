Tasty momo recipe

Ingredients:

Here are the essential ingredients for making tasty momo:

  • Dough : 2 cups of all purpose flour
  • Water as needed
  • 1/2 tea spoon salt
  • Filllings: 500g of chicken & cabbage
  • 1 small onion , finely chopped
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • 4 green chilli
  • Salt and pepper , soya sauce
  • Momo masala

Directions:

  1. Prepare the dough: in a bowl mix the flour with salt . Gradually add water and knead until u form a smooth dough and cover & rest it until 30 mins.
  2. Prepare the fillings: In another bowl combine chicken , cabbage , ginger garlic paste and add soya sauce & green chilli.
  3. Divide the dough into small balls and roll it until it becomes thin circular disc.
  4. Add a spoon full of feelings in the circular disc and fold the edges as per your choice.
  5. Steam all the momos for around 15-30 mins.
